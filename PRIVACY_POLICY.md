# Privacy Policy

## Vaultix

Vaultix is an offline privacy-focused personal vault application developed by AI Genius Partners.

---

## Data Collection

Vaultix does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data.

The app works completely offline.

---

## No Cloud Storage

Vaultix does not use:

- Cloud servers
- External databases
- Online accounts
- User tracking
- Analytics services

All user data remains stored locally on the user's own device.

---

## Security

Vaultix provides local PIN-based protection and offline storage features to help users protect sensitive information on their devices.

Users are responsible for keeping their device secure and remembering their PIN and recovery information.

---

## Third-Party Services

Vaultix does not integrate with third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.
